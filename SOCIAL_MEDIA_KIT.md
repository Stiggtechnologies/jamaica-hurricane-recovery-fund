## Image Guidelines

### Photo Requirements
- High resolution (minimum 1920x1080px for web)
- Focus on: community rebuilding, hope, resilience, Jamaican culture
- Include diverse representation
- Show both challenges and solutions
- Authentic documentary-style preferred

### Recommended Image Sources
1. **Pexels**: Search "Jamaica," "community building," "Caribbean," "volunteers"
2. **Unsplash**: Search similar terms
3. **Project Photos**: Real images from JHRF projects (when available)

### Image Categories Needed
- Before/after rebuilding photos
- Volunteer activities
- Community gatherings
- Construction progress
- Beneficiary stories
- Founder and team photos
- Jamaican landscapes and culture
